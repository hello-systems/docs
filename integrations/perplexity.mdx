## Connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Integrations → Perplexity" />
  <Step title="Enter your Perplexity API key" />
  <Step title="Done" />
</Steps>

## Actions

| Action | Example Prompt |
|--------|---------------|
| Search | `Use @Perplexity to find recent news about {{company}}` |
| Research | `Use @Perplexity to answer: What is {{company}}'s market position?` |
| Fact-check | `Use @Perplexity to verify these claims` |

## Example

```
Use @Perplexity to research {{company}}:
- Recent funding or announcements
- Key executives and their backgrounds
- Main competitors
- Recent news (last 30 days)

Cite sources.
```
