## Connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Integrations → HubSpot" />
  <Step title="Click Connect and authorize Script" />
  <Step title="Select your HubSpot account" />
</Steps>

## Actions

| Action | Example Prompt |
|--------|---------------|
| Get contacts | `Get contacts from @HubSpot where status = "lead"` |
| Create contact | `Create contact in @HubSpot with {{data}}` |
| Update deal | `Update @HubSpot deal stage to "Qualified"` |
| Add note | `Add note to @HubSpot contact` |
