## Connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Integrations → Apollo" />
  <Step title="Enter your Apollo API key" />
  <Step title="Done" />
</Steps>

## Actions

| Action | Example Prompt |
|--------|---------------|
| Enrich company | `Get company info from @Apollo for {{domain}}` |
| Find contacts | `Find decision makers at @Apollo for {{company}}` |
| Enrich person | `Get person details from @Apollo for {{email}}` |

## Example

```
For each lead:
- Get company info from @Apollo
- Find VP-level contacts
- Get direct emails and phone numbers
- Calculate company score
```
