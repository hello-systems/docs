## Connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Integrations → Airtable" />
  <Step title="Click Connect and authorize Script" />
  <Step title="Select your bases" />
</Steps>

## Actions

| Action | Example Prompt |
|--------|---------------|
| Get records | `Get all records from @Airtable "Leads" base` |
| Filter | `Get records where Status = "New" from @Airtable` |
| Create | `Create record in @Airtable with {{data}}` |
| Update | `Update record in @Airtable where Email = "{{email}}"` |
