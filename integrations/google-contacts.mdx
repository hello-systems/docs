## Actions

| Action | Example Prompt |
|--------|----------------|
| Get contacts | `Get all @Google Contacts` |
| Create contact | `Create @Google Contacts entry for {{name}} with email {{email}}` |
| Update contact | `Update @Google Contacts {{id}} with new phone number` |
| Search contacts | `Search @Google Contacts for "{{query}}"` |
