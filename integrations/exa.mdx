## Connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Integrations → Exa" />
  <Step title="Enter your Exa API key" />
  <Step title="Done" />
</Steps>

## Actions

| Action | Example Prompt |
|--------|---------------|
| Search | `Use @Exa to find articles about {{topic}}` |
| Find similar | `Use @Exa to find pages similar to {{url}}` |
| Research | `Use @Exa to find the top 10 sources for {{query}}` |

## Example

```
Use @Exa to find:
- 10 most relevant articles about {{company}} AI strategy
- Include title, URL, and summary
- Published in last 6 months
```
